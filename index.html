What is Stay (2005) about?

Exactly.

What? I'm hearing you say, but I mean it. That's EXACTLY what the movie is about.

I'm the kind of spoiler-phobic person who preffers not to even watch previews before I watch a film - too often the entire story is summarized in what is supposed to be a sneak peak. However, with the movie Stay it is absolutely NECISSARY to go in blind, knowing little or nothing about what the story is, and what is supposed to be happening. I so enjoyed this movie because it's surrealism left me just slightly suspicious something strange was going on, and I spent the entire movie trying to figure out what was going on.

What is Stay about? Stay is about figuring out what Stay is about. And I love it for that.

But really...
I want to know what this movie is about.
